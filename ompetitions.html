The UEFA Champions League is an annual club football competition organized by the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA). It is one of the most prestigious tournaments in world football, featuring the best clubs from European countries. The competition involves a group stage, followed by knockout rounds, culminating in the final match. The group stage consists of 32 teams, divided into eight groups of four. Each team plays six matches in the group stage, facing each other home and away.
